== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `teams` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'teams' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to teams to customize this plugin.
4. Finally Enjoy it.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==


= Can i display Unlimited Team Members on my website with this team plugin? =

Yes, you can add display Unlimited Team Members on your websites on any pages or posts on in sidebar , you can easily change their layout position through drag and drop them. 

= How can i add teams on page or post?  =

Go to Teams menu  teams now copy your team shortcode [TEAM_M id=xyz] (xyz is your shortcode id) , paste it in your page or post.


= Can i add my own custom css for my team?  =

Using custom css you can apply your own custom design on frontend.

= Can i add social profiles for team members? =

Yes, you can easily add your team members social profiles - Facebook, twitter linked-in.

= How to add Team in widget area ?  =  

Just open your widget menu then add "Text widget" , now paste your Team shortcode [TEAM_B id=xx] (here xx your shortcode id) in this text widget. 

= How can I edit designs?  =  

Go to Teams menu add or edit any teams, now select your designs, change settings for this design like color, font size etc..
